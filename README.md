# E-Commerce-Store-Sales-Report
# Problem Statement
The growth of online shopping generates large amounts of sales data, making it difficult for businesses to analyze performance, customer behavior, and product demand. Without proper data visualization, it is hard to identify top products, profitable categories, payment methods, and regional sales trends. Therefore, an interactive E-Commerce Store Sales Report dashboard using Microsoft Power BI is needed to visualize sales, profit, quantity, and regional performance to support better business decision-making.
# Result
1.Total Sales, Profit, Quantity (Cards):
The dashboard shows overall business performance with Total Sales = 438K, Total Profit = 37K, and Total Quantity Sold = 5615.
2.Total Sales by Category (Donut Chart):
The highest sales come from Electronics (166K), followed by Clothing (144K) and Furniture (127K).
3.Sales & Profit by Sub-Category (Bar Chart):
This chart compares total sales and profit for each product sub-category to identify the most profitable items.
4.Top 5 Customers (Column Chart):
Shows top customers such as Harivansh and Madhav who generated the highest sales.
5.Sales by Payment Mode (Pie Chart):
COD is the most used payment method, followed by Credit Card, EMI, UPI, and Debit Card.
6.Top States & Cities (Charts):
Maharashtra and Indore generate the highest sales, indicating strong regional demand.
